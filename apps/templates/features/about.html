SERVERIN

Serverin - is a platform by the students and for the students. We aim to provide a user-friendly platform to enhance development and integration skills.

What we do

Change with an AIM

Serverin is a platform for the students to leverage themselves in an interactive development workspace. We not only aim at providing the services but also increases the adaptability of acquring new skills. This also avails user to work efficiently in Agile Environment.

Serverin is an integrated Umbrella of DevOps tools. We have used DevOps tools like - Jenkins , Kubernetes, Ansible, Terraform, Docker, Amazon Web services. We are also integrating MLOps as a service. Together we face the challenges and celebrate the victories.
